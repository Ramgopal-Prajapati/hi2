Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about High-Tech Institute

What are the admission requirements?

Most of our courses require basic computer knowledge and 10+2 education. Some advanced courses may have additional prerequisites which are listed on the course pages.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes, we offer flexible payment options including EMI plans for most courses. Contact our admissions team to discuss payment options that work for you.

What is the class schedule?

We offer multiple batches throughout the day including morning, afternoon, and evening classes. Weekend batches are also available for working professionals.

Do you provide placement assistance?

Yes, we have a dedicated placement cell that provides resume building, interview preparation, and connects students with our hiring partners.

Can I attend a demo class?

Absolutely! We encourage prospective students to attend a free demo class to experience our teaching methodology. Use the form above to schedule one.

What if I miss a class?

We provide recorded sessions for all classes and allow students to attend the same class in a different batch to make up for missed sessions.
